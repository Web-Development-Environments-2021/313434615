About Me

My name is Lia Weisberger, I was born in 1926 in Finland at the same year as the queen Elizabeth.
I married Shlomo in 1956 and we have three wonderfull kids Anat, Eytan and Gil. I have nine grandchildren Yoav, Lior, Amit, Roni, Roy, Shahar, Guy, Nir and Talya.
I also have 5 great grandchildren Uri, Rani, Shir, Ron and Lior.
